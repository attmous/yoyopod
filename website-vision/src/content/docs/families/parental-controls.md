## What parents control

- The contact whitelist: who can call and be called, who can exchange voice notes
- The content library: which music and stories are on the device
- Location sharing: on or off, and who in the family sees the map
- Quiet hours: when the device goes calm for school or sleep (TBD)
- Device basics: name, volume limits (TBD), and software updates

## Where you set it

- Everything lives in the [parent app](/apps/parent-app/) — no controls buried on the device
- Contacts: add, remove, and name whitelist entries from the app
- Library: load and remove music and stories, build playlists (flow TBD)
- Location: sharing toggle and settings, per family member (TBD)
- Changes reach the device over its mobile connection (sync timing TBD)

## What kids can change themselves

- What plays next: choosing music and stories within the loaded library
- Who to call: any contact on the whitelist, anytime it's allowed
- Volume within the parent-set ceiling (TBD)
- Sending a check-in from the device
- Nothing else — no settings maze on the canvas, on purpose
