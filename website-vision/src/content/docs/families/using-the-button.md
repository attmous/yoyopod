## What you'll need

- The device, powered on and paired
- Five minutes with the kid — no manual, no menus to memorize
- This page's one-line summary: one button, three gestures, one wheel

## Steps

- Meet the Hub: the home wheel that carries the screens — Hub, Listen, Talk, Setup
- Tap: the everyday gesture (exact per-screen mapping TBD)
- Double-tap: the second gesture (exact per-screen mapping TBD)
- Hold: the special one — hold doubles as push-to-talk for voice messages
- Walk the wheel once together: from Hub into [Listen](/apps/listen/), into [Talk](/apps/talk/), and back
- Let the kid drive: parent narrates, kid presses

## Tips

- Teach hold-to-talk like a walkie-talkie: hold, speak, release
- There is nothing to break — every path leads back to the Hub
- The glass stays calm on purpose: one small screen, no feed, no notifications to chase
