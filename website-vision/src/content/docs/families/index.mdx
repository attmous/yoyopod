## How this guide is organized

Three parts, in reading order:

1. **Getting Started** — the first hour: unboxing, pairing the parent app,
   and teaching the one button.
2. **Everyday Use** — the four things the device does daily: listening,
   talking, location, and staying charged.
3. **Safety & Privacy** — the controls parents hold and the promises we
   make about data.

A FAQ catches everything that doesn't fit.
