## What we are not

- Not a smartphone replacement
- Not a toy
- Not a screen-first device
- Not an addictive gadget
- For each item: one short paragraph on what the temptation is and why we refuse it

## What we will not market

- Never "AI for kids"
- Never "a new communication platform"
- Never "a smart wearable"
- Never "an educational device"
- For each label: why the framing sells short-term and corrodes the product long-term

## Why we write this down

- Refusals as strategy: what a product will not become is as load-bearing as what it will
- A public checklist parents can hold us to — and a filter for feature requests and partnerships
- How this page ties back to the [Product Principles](/company/principles/) and the [Roadmap](/company/roadmap/)
- The category we do claim instead: "the first device before a smartphone"
- Keeping the list stable: additions are cheap, removals must be near-impossible (governance TBD)
