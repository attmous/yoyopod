## How this section is organized

Four pages, and the order matters: the **mission** states the promise —
*safer independence for kids, peace of mind for parents, before the
smartphone*. The **principles** turn that promise into rules a feature must
pass. **What yoyopod Is Not** is the same thing stated negatively — the
anti-positioning list as a first-class page, because for this product the
refusals *are* the strategy. The **roadmap** puts dates and rounds on it.
