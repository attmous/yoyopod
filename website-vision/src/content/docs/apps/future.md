## How ideas earn a place here

- V1 pillars come first: whitelist calls and voice messages, live-ish location, local-first listening, the parent app
- Every idea must pass the [company principles](/company/principles/) before it gets a slot
- Screen-light test: does it add calm audio value without pulling the kid's eyes to the glass?
- Parent-managed test: does it keep parents in charge without turning them into wardens?
- This page is a parking lot, not a roadmap — nothing here is committed

## Parked ideas

- Audio messages to a wider circle of parent-approved family members (TBD)
- Walkie-talkie style groups among already-whitelisted contacts (TBD)
- Audiobooks alongside music and stories in the local library (TBD)
- All of the above stay whitelist-only and parent-managed, and wait until the V1 pillars are solid

## Explicitly rejected

- Not a smartphone replacement — and not one feature at a time drifting into becoming one
- No camera, no browser, no app store: permanent decisions, not pending features
- Not "AI for kids", not a new communication platform, not a smart wearable, not an educational device
- Not a toy, not screen-first, never engagement-optimized
- The full stance lives at [what we are not](/company/what-we-are-not/)
