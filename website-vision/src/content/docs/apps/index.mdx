*yoyopod ships a handful of deliberately small applications. This section
describes each one as a product experience — what it does, how its flows
feel, and where it runs.*

## How this section is organized

Six applications plus a parking lot. The first four carry the device's own
screen names (Hub is the home wheel that leads to them): **Listen**,
**Talk**, **Ask**, and **Locate** live on the device; the **Parent App**
lives on the parent's phone; **Setup** is the on-device onboarding that
binds the two.
[What Comes Next](/apps/future/) holds vision ideas that are compatible with
what yoyopod refuses to become.

Every app page shares the same five headings: *What it is · Key flows · On
the device · In the parent app · Status today* — so you can compare apps
column by column.
