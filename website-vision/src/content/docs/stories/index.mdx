*Before any page explains how yoyopod works, these six pages show what it is
**for**. Each story is one persona, one moment, one V1 pillar made concrete.*

## What a story page is

Every story follows the same four-part shape: **The moment** (a scene from
real family life) → **What yoyopod does** (the product experience, in plain
words) → **Behind the scenes** (which applications and platform pieces carry
it) → **Why it matters** (the independence-for-kids, reassurance-for-parents
promise it fulfills). Stories are the bridge between the family guide and
the builders guide: each one links both ways.
